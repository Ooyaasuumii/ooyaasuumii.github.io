Who is CR Concrete?


CR Concrete, operated and owned by Carlos Ricardo, is a trusted name in residential and commercial concrete services. Having been in business for over 30 years, we are known throughout Central Ohio as a small, but experienced, group of contractors. Our primary services include tearing out driveways, patios, and walkways and replacing them with stamped concrete or exposed aggregate. However, we are capable of much more. We encourage all potential clients to reach out via phone or email to discuss our services. We offer free estimates and guarantee the quality of our work. Referrals are also available upon request.
